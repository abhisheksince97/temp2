About Us

Who are we?

Sustainable, Contemporary and Renewable Initiatives for Transforming India (SUKRITI) is a not-for-profit company in collaboration with Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Roorkee, working towards social and economic development of the country through sustainable engineering initiatives and innovations.


What we do?

We are a group of IIT graduates forevisioning a dream of clean and green future of India. We are working to change the present sanitary conditions to improve the penetration of alternative renewable energy, to develop products for better governance, low cost housing, skill development and healthcare



  • Strenghten India on global map
  • Make India an energy surplus country
  • Reach out to each and every house
  • Match infastrusture of the future
  • User centric design and application
 

Our Model

Vaccum Flushing

Hygienic, power-saving and less water intensive. Vacuum flushing is better at odor removal, consumes only around 2 Watts of power per flush, and requires less than 1 liter per flush.

Grey Water Recycling

Treated grey water is reused for flushing and cleaning purposes. A grey-water treatment system makes it a win-win for water and energy use.

Black Water Treatment

The existing treatment technologies suffer from drawbacks because they don’t take the human factor into the equation. Our toilet simply treats the black water, thus eliminating the need for manual cleaning. No harmful effluents into the environment.

Green

Technology to alleviate problems should not be built at the expense of the planet. Our toilet uses solar panels to cater to the electrical needs of the system.

Miscellaneous

If our toilet were a woman, it would be the ideal woman. It’s low maintenance and it expects nothing from the user. Non-slippery floors, sensors for user-detection, auto-flush and floor swiping and hydrophobic surfaces – it uses design for all the right reasons.

Urine Processing

Urine diverting squatting pans make it all the more easy to treat black water. Nitrogen and Phosphorous rich salts extracted from on-site reactors are a feedstock for the fertilizer industry.

Toilet Positioning App

A dedicated mobile app for discovering & locating nearby toilet, with feedback mechanism.

Waterless Odourless Urinals

Zero odour - No water Urinals with zero energy intake and no recurring expenditures.

Innovative Superstructure

Our toilet is pre-fabricated and so, easily installable. Designed to maximize ventilation by properly engineered super-structure, it also scores well on the green front with its renewable technology.
